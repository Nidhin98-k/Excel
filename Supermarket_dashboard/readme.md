Super Market Sales Performance Dashboard

Created an interactive dashboard using a Super Market Sales dataset in Microsoft Excel. It contains KPI Cards, Pivot Charts, and Slicers to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product line trends.

KPI Cards: Total Sales, Gross Income, Quantity Sold, Average Customer Rating, and Top Selling Product Line.
Pivot Charts: Monthly Sales Trend, Sales by Product Line, Gross Income by Product Line, Sales by City, Sales by Customer Type, Sales by Payment Method, and Sales by Branch.
Slicers: Months, Product Line, City, and Payment Method.

The dashboard helps visualize sales trends and provides insights into product performance, customer preferences, and business operations.
